Child Safety Standards

Our app strictly prohibits Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

  • We have published standards against CSAE on this page.
  • We provide an in-app mechanism for user feedback and reports.
  • We act on any reports of CSAE and remove content accordingly.
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.
  • For any type of CSAE-type of issues, the reference-email for contact of us is ucacecyt@gmail.com
