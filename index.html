Child Safety Standards

Our app strictly prohibits Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

  • We have published standards against CSAE (this page)
  • We provide an in-app mechanism for user feedback and reports
  • We act promptly on any reports of CSAE and remove content accordingly
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations

✉️ For any reports or concerns regarding CSAE, please contact us immediately at: innotec.cyt@uc.edu.py
